Motto

Out of Many,

One People

The rich Jamaican culture was formulated from a mixture of different ethnic groups that came to the Island over number of years in the past. Ethnic groups included the Chinese, Indians, Africans, Spanish, English. Each group took with them their own culture, ways of doing things such as language, dance/entertainment, dress, hunting, farming/agriculture, archiutecture and food and food preparation.

The wide diversity of cultures merged into one formed the little exceptional island of jamaica. Different groups, but one, hence the motto - out of many one people.
