Retrieval Practice

Theoty is good to become grounded in the fundamentals. However, to truly learn a concept, it needs to be stored in long term memory. To help with this, simply re-reading or re-watching a video is not very effective. When we are forced to recal is when we recognized what we really know. At times we may forget something, but this is perfectly normal and the purpose of the retrieval practice to then go back to the material to help aid recollection, hence reenforcing concept in long-term memory.

I am Adventurous, So!

I love The Outdoors

Dunn's River Falls was my backyard while growing up so I would visit multiple times per month with my friends, especially during the holidays. The falls is magnificent and the water is rather cold. On each visit I would run up the falls atleast five times which was quite the work out. Dunn's River is an extremely delightful experience you have to enjoy atleast once. There are many other other spots to enjoy outdoors, such as White River rafting, ATV riding, ziplining, river tubing and horseback riding at Chuckka. I believe in enjoying the beauty of my Island, its not just for Tourists. There are numberous places to visit, to name a few: Blue Hole, Mystic Mountain, Reach Falls, 7 Mile Beach in Negril, YS Falls, Doctors Cave Beach, Green Grotto Cave, Bob Marley Museum, Rose Hall Great House and many more... Many of which I have been before and looking forward to visit the others soon.

I'm A Foodie

I am fairly adventurous when it comes to food. I love trying new types of food, but I am easily turned off if food is not properly prepared. Presentation is also a plus but not my first concern in certain situations. Food should be seasoned and cooked properly. Jamaica is well known for having a great food such as Ackee and saltfish, Jerked Chicken and the Patties. There is also great street food options by the locals at apecific spots or the pushcart soup vendors. Different coultures such as the Chineese, Indians and Jewish along with other ethinic gorups have also incorporated their traditional dishes with a Jamaican twist. All in all, great food is on the Island.
