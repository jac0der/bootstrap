Retrieval Practice

Theoty is good to become grounded in the fundamentals. However, to truly learn a concept, it needs to be stored in long term memory. To help with this, simply re-reading or re-watching a video is not very effective. When we are forced to recal is when we recognized what we really know. At times we may forget something, but this is perfectly normal and the purpose of the retrieval practice to then go back to the material to help aid recollection, hence reenforcing concept in long-term memory.
